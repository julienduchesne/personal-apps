# Future Ideas

## Fetch new recipes from the web

Discover and suggest new recipes by scraping or querying recipe websites/APIs. Could integrate with the suggestion engine to recommend recipes based on user preferences and past meals.

## Grocery store rebates integration

Fetch current rebates/deals from nearby grocery store websites. Use the deals to influence meal planning for the upcoming week — prioritizing meals that use ingredients currently on sale. This could introduce a new "grocery" concept to plan a trip to the grocery store, including a shopping list optimized around active promotions.
